# 关于自然常数 e

$$
\Huge
\begin{align*}
    e & = \lim_{n \to \infty} \left(1 + \frac{1}{n}\right)^n
        = \lim_{t \to 0} (1 + t)^\frac{1}{t} \\
      & = 2.71828182845904523536... \\
\end{align*}
$$

## 欧拉公式

$$
\Huge
\begin{align*}
  e^{i \pi}
    & = \cos x + i \sin x \\
    & = \sum^\infty_{n = 0} \frac{(i \pi)^n}{n!} \\
    & = -1 \\
\end{align*}
$$
